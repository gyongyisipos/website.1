IS EVERY PICTURE

WORTH 1,000 WORDS?
COUNTER-ARGUMENTS
  • We all know the power of words.
  • We know that a handful of well-chosen words will say more than a waterfall of clichés.
  • We all know that one precise word can say more than 1,000 stock images.
Images and words are different forms of language. One can express that which the other cannot. The key to a strong presentation is to use them together and complement their strengths.
Dos and Don'ts for Achieving Balance
  1. Prioritize Information
  2. Choose visuals that tell a story
  3. Be careful when adding one picture after the other
  4. Rehearse to Get a Feeling for the Impact of the Images
  5. Ask Others How They Feel About Your Visuals

SUMMARY
If you don't have a meaningful picture, don't use a placeholder just to add color.
Choose anything but the best means to communicate disrespect to your audience.
One carefully selected photo is more powerful than 1,000 stock images, just like three carefully considered words say more than 1,000 words of fluff.
